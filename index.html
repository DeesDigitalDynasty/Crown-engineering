About Us

At Crown Engineering, we are at the forefront of marine infrastructure development, offering specialized services in port dredging, land reclamation, environmental remediation, and coastal engineering. Our work is driven by a passion for sustainable solutions and a commitment to excellence that consistently delivers results across Nigeria’s coastal and inland waterways.

Backed by a dedicated team of 23 skilled professionals, our workforce includes engineers, technicians, and field specialists who bring decades of hands-on experience to every project. The company is structured into three core departments. Dredging & Marine Operations, Civil & Coastal Engineering, and Environmental Management, each led by seasoned section heads who ensure technical precision and smooth project execution.

Whether we're deepening harbors, reclaiming land for new developments, or protecting vulnerable shorelines from erosion, our mission is to shape resilient coastal communities through innovation, safety, and reliable service delivery. At Crown Engineering, we don’t just move the earth, we shape the future of marine engineering.
